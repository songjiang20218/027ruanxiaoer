Enable 2FA for Google
Logged into your Google account, click on your account icon at the top right of the screen. Then click Manage your Google Account. Website： https://google.com/
Step 1: Locate the 2FA settings first
① Account ② Security ③ 2-Step Verification
Step 2: Enable Authentication App
Enable Authentication app Setup 2FA.
Step 3: Add account to Authenticator App
"Scan QR code" or "Enter manually", or you can take a sceenshot of QR code, and "Import from Photo" to add account to Authenticator App.
Step 4: Verify your Code
Return to Google App and enter the Code to verify.
When you're all set up,you can safely use 2FA now.
